Coffee and Code



Code is Life


As an IT student, coding is an inevitable part of my life and I find myself in a perplexing love-hate relationship with it. The syntax and intricate problem-solving involved in computer programming can be daunting, yet I remind myself that nothing worth having comes easy, as I was taught.


Coffee is Lifer


Amidst the array of indispensable tools that aid programmers in their craft, coffee reigns supreme as a faithful companion. Along with the likes of ChatGPT, Stack Overflow, YouTube, and Google, of course. Research even suggests that coffee can enhance one's focus and provide a much needed "pick-me-up" during those drowsy workdays. I have observed my senior colleagues diligently sipping their coffee, and it dawned on me that the richer and darker their brew, the greater their prowess at coding.
