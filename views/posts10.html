Street Food Break



Break utilization


Without fail, every morning at 9 A.M., the company's trusted vendors make their way to our doors, offering a mouthwatering selection of street food delicacies such as qwek-qwek, fishballs, and tempura. Interestingly, the company has a laid-back attitude towards interns when it comes to taking breaks. Nevertheless, I have come to realize the importance of utilizing my time wisely during my 15 min respite. Apart from indulging in a quick bite, I make it a point to converse with my colleagues, seek assistance on challenging tasks, and clarify any uncertainties I may have.
